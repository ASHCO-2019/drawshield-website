--- pageTitle: "Feedback on reported errors" ---

On this page I will discuss any errors that are reported to me and explain whether they are indeed errors; or whether they are just shortcomings in the program or misunderstandings of blazonry.

October 04, 2017

Don't understand " Quarterly [of] [six] [the]"

Just list the quarters, e.g. "Quartered first, azure; second, or; third, gules; fourth, sable; fifth, argent; sixth, murrey.". I will modify the program to understand the "of six", and "the" in front of the quarters.

September 24, 2017

Don't understand words in brackets - Gules two [pales] [or]

For more than one Pale use the term 'palets' - you can have up to six (but this looks like a lot like 'paly' so there isn't much point!). So for the intended effect use 'Gules two palets or'.

September 19, 2017
