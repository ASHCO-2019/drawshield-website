Drawshield 2.5 Release - Feature 3: Printing

Again, under the new options... section is a checkbox called "Make Printable". This causes the shield to be created in a plain new tab, and considerably larger. This makes it ideal for printing!

And what is more, selecting the colour scheme Outlines Only creates a monochrome version, ideal for creating "colouring pages" for children of all ages!

Comments and suggestions for further improvements welcome...
