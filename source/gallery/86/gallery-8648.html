    Quarterly or crusilly gules and gules crusilly or,
a bordure, a cross quarter pierced and 5 lozenges
 palewise in saltire all counterchanged

Suggested by an anonymous user, who adds:

Quarterly (party per cross) and crusilly (seme of crosses), charged with a cross, lozenges in saltire (technically a cross bendwise) and a bordure for balance, all counterchanged.
