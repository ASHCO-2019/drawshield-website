    gu
a cross or voided
in dexter chief two lion passant guardant or langued
 armed blue in pale

Notes

The arms of Normandy are gu two lion passant guardant or langued armed azure when the English lions/leopards derive. The Nordic Cross or voided in gu is called the St-Olaf Cross. The were combined in the 1970's as the emblem of a Norman political movement.
