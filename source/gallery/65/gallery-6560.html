    per pale embattled lozengy sable and argent and crusilly
 vert and argent  on a bend  maily or and sanguine
 3 dice bendwise sinister crimson  in third quarter
 a compass star  white  in second quarter a compass
 rose  white

Notes

Includes the crests of the families Wismar, Medici, and Cortes
